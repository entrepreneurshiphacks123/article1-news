  China's Ministry of Justice declared the European Union's foreign subsidies
  investigation into security-screening firm Nuctech Co. an improper exercise of
  extraterritorial jurisdiction and ordered Chinese parties not to comply with
  the measures, according to Bloomberg Politics. The ministry's directive marks
  one of the first formal Chinese government orders barring domestic firms from
  cooperating with the EU's Foreign Subsidies Regulation, a tool Brussels has
  increasingly deployed against state-backed competitors.
