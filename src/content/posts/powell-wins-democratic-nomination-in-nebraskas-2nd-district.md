  Political organizer Denise Powell won the Democratic primary in Nebraska's 2nd
  Congressional District, defeating state Sen. John Cavanaugh 39% to 37% with

  by EMILY's List and the Congressional Black Caucus, will face Republican
  Brinker Harding in November in a seat Cook Political Report rates "Lean
  Democrat" — one of just three House districts a Republican won in 2024 that
  Kamala Harris also carried.
