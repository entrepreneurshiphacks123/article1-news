  Rep. Steve Cohen, D-Tenn., announced he will not seek re-election after
  Tennessee's Republican-led legislature and Gov. Bill Lee enacted a map
  dividing his majority-Black 9th District three ways, according to NBC News.
  Said Cohen: "Last week Tennessee Republicans silenced the Black vote here in
  Memphis to make Republican victories likely." Cohen's retirement is the 59th
  by a sitting House member this cycle, the second-highest total since 1930, per
  NBC News analysis.
