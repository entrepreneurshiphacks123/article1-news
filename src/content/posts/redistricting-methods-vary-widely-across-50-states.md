  redistricting, while nine use independent or politician commissions and two
  others employ hybrid systems, according to Ballotpedia. The single-member
  district requirement now standard in every state was not made permanently
  mandatory by Congress until 1967, with the passage of the Uniform
  Congressional District Act — 125 years after the Apportionment Act of 1842
  first imposed it.
