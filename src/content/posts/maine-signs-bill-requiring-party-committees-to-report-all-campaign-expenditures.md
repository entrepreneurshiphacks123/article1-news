  disclose all expenditures made to influence a campaign and updating

  according to Ballotpedia News. The Legislature also failed to expand
  ranked-choice voting to state general elections after the Maine Supreme
  Judicial Court ruled the proposal unconstitutional, finding it conflicts with
  the state constitution's plurality-winner requirement.
