  The House voted 212-212 on a Democratic resolution that would have directed
  President Trump to remove U.S. forces from hostilities with Iran within 30
  days, according to CBS News. Three Republicans — Reps. Thomas Massie, Brian
  Fitzpatrick, and Tom Barrett — voted in favor; Rep. Jared Golden of Maine was
  the lone Democrat opposed. Said Rep. Josh Gottheimer, the resolution's
  sponsor: "I didn't want to have to bring this resolution to the floor. I had
  hoped that the administration would have changed course after I introduced it
  and properly briefed the Congress and the country."
